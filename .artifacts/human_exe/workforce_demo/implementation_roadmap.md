# Workforce AI Implementation Roadmap

## 30-day (Week 1-4)
Owners: Transformation Lead, Data Lead
- Assessment and data foundation | Owner: Data Lead | Gate: DG-30
  - Deliverables: baseline workforce dataset, data quality report
  - Exit criteria: critical data gaps documented, governance intake approved
  - Risks: source system inconsistency

## 60-day (Week 5-8)
Owners: Transformation Lead, L&D
- AI literacy and workflow redesign | Owner: L&D | Gate: DG-60
  - Deliverables: role learning pathways, workflow redesign backlog
  - Exit criteria: pilot candidate workflows signed-off
  - Risks: manager bandwidth

## 90-day (Week 9-12)
Owners: AI Product Owner, Supervisor Team
- Pilot selection and proof of value | Owner: AI Product Owner | Gate: DG-90
  - Deliverables: pilot charter, measurement dashboard
  - Exit criteria: pilot KPI baseline established
  - Risks: limited adoption

## 6-month (Month 4-6)
Owners: AI Product Owner, Governance Council
- Supervised AI Agent buildout | Owner: AI Product Owner | Gate: DG-6M
  - Deliverables: agent playbooks, approval gate matrix
  - Exit criteria: policy compliance above 99%
  - Risks: integration complexity

## 12-month (Month 7-12)
Owners: Transformation Office, Department Leads
- Department deployment and enterprise scale | Owner: Transformation Office | Gate: DG-12M
  - Deliverables: department playbooks, continuous improvement cadence
  - Exit criteria: target KPI attainment trend positive
  - Risks: change fatigue
